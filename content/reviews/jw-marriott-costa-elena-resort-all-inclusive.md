The Guanacaste province of Costa Rica has long been a whispered secret among those who seek nature's embrace without sacrificing refinement—a landscape where the Pacific's rhythm dictates the pace of life, and verdant forests meet pristine, remote beaches. Within this captivating canvas, the Costa Elena development emerges as a thoughtful sanctuary, poised to welcome a new beacon of luxury hospitality. Here, the JW Marriott Costa Elena Resort All-Inclusive is not merely opening its doors in 2026—it is debuting a significant chapter for the brand, marking its inaugural all-inclusive property within the vibrant Caribbean and Latin American expanse. This 415-room resort promises a redefinition of the all-inclusive model, shifting it from mere convenience to a deeply immersive, wellness-focused journey. It is an ambitious undertaking, aligning with Marriott International's broader strategic vision to elevate the premium all-inclusive segment, yet it remains firmly rooted in the JW Marriott ethos of understated elegance and genuine care. The anticipation surrounding its arrival speaks to a desire for experiences that are both effortless and profoundly enriching, set against a backdrop of unparalleled natural beauty.

The architectural narrative of JW Marriott Costa Elena is intrinsically woven into the fabric of its surroundings. Designers have approached the 415-room footprint with a quiet reverence for the land, ensuring that structures emerge organically from the topography rather than imposing upon it. Expect a design language that celebrates biophilia, where expansive open-air lobbies and suites blur the lines between interior comfort and the wild, untamed beauty just beyond. Tactile materials—locally sourced hardwoods, hand-chiseled stone, and artisanal textiles—will form the sensory palette, inviting guests to connect with the region's heritage through touch and sight. Each design detail, from the subtle curve of a pathway to the placement of a window framing a specific ocean vista, is meticulously considered, fostering a sense of calm and belonging. The resort will not simply exist within Costa Elena—it will become an extension of it, a harmonious blend of human ingenuity and natural splendor, echoing the quiet strength of the Guanacaste landscape.

At the heart of the JW Marriott Costa Elena experience lies a profound commitment to wellness, interpreted not as a fleeting trend but as an integrated philosophy of living. This all-inclusive model transcends the typical buffet lines and scheduled activities, instead curating a holistic journey for mind, body, and spirit. Imagine mornings beginning with sunrise yoga overlooking the Pacific, followed by nutrient-rich, locally sourced cuisine that nourishes without compromise. The resort’s wellness programming will likely extend to immersive spa treatments drawing on indigenous ingredients, guided nature walks through the surrounding dry tropical forest, and opportunities for mindful reflection in secluded garden enclaves. The beauty of this reimagined all-inclusive lies in its seamless delivery—every element, from the quality of the ingredients to the expertise of the wellness practitioners, is elevated, allowing guests to fully surrender to rejuvenation without the constant consideration of individual transactions. It is a luxury of effortless well-being, where every choice feels intentional and every moment contributes to a deeper sense of peace.

The signature JW Marriott touch—a blend of sophisticated comfort and intuitive service—will undoubtedly define the guest experience at Costa Elena. This is a brand built on a legacy of quiet luxury, where attention to detail is paramount and genuine hospitality is expressed through thoughtful gestures rather than ostentatious displays. Guests can anticipate a level of personalized care that anticipates needs before they are voiced, delivered by a team deeply attuned to the nuances of refined service. From the moment of arrival, a sense of calm will envelop visitors, allowing them to shed the demands of the outside world. The 415 rooms and suites, while offering contemporary amenities, will be sanctuaries of tranquility, designed with natural light, comfortable furnishings, and private outdoor spaces that invite contemplation. This is where the trusted name of JW Marriott truly shines—in its ability to create an environment where every aspect of the stay, from the culinary offerings to the curated excursions, is executed with an unwavering commitment to excellence, fostering a truly restorative escape.

As it prepares to welcome its first guests in 2026, this resort stands as a promise—a promise of a sanctuary where the wild beauty of Costa Rica meets the refined elegance of JW Marriott, creating an experience that is both deeply personal and effortlessly luxurious.
